OUR ACTIVITIES

The IT Community Club annually hosts events in IT, AI, development, security, and computer science to enhance participants' skills and share knowledge.

ITC TOUR

the ITC Tour comprises a series of events where we impart our expertise in Information Technology and Communication to various clubs and organizations Through a series of workshops.

ITC TOUR JUNIOR

The "ITC Tour Junior" is a series of educational outings for high school students,It involves visits to secondary schools to learn about different academic specialties offered there,It also includes presentations on university majors to help students understand their options for future studies and careers.

ITC REVISION

ITC Revision at our club involves members assisting each other during exam periods.Those with expertise in specific subjects offer valuable help and guidance to their peers ensuring everyone is well-prepared and supported throughout their studies

ITC FORMATION

Training sessions in our club involve structured educational programs where members receive guidance on different subjects, aimed at enhancing their skills and knowledge in specific areas.

ITC CUP CHALLENGE

is a dynamic competition among club members within our university and from other universities It's a spirited event where participants showcase skills,teamwork,and friendly rivalries across campuses.
